Bio

Justin Dorfman is Sticker Mule's Director of Developer Relations and is responsible for championing the needs of Open Source developers.

Justin started BootstrapCDN in 2012 and is heavily involved with the FOSS community contributing to Bootstrap, Font Awesome, Grunt, Ionic, jQuery Foundation, Twemoji, Nginx & GNU Bash. He is also on the Glucosio Foundation's Advisory Board.

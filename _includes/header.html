Justin Dorfman

Open Source Sustainer

Justin Dorfman is Reblaze's Open Source Program Manager and is responsible for fostering the adoption of their project Curiefense in the cloud-native space. Previously, Justin led similar initiatives for Gitcoin, Sticker Mule, & MaxCDN.

Justin has contributed to Bootstrap, Font Awesome, jQuery, Nginx, GNU Bash, and many more. He also serves on the Selection Committee for Mozilla's Open Source Support (MOSS) program, as well as the Open Source Collective's board of directors. In 2017, he co-founded SustainOSS, which hosts events and podcasts for Open Source Software Sustainers.
